Condition of participation

- To have a chance to participate to this give-away, you must do a purchase on the website. After the purchase you'll receive a code that you must put on the field below on this page to be validated as a participant.


- Once your registration done, you'll now just have to wait until we draw the winners of this give-away. The draw will happens on the 10th of January 2022.


- After the draw, if you're one of our lucky winner, you'll receive a mail with a form to complete with your informations. This form will allows us to send you the prize of the give-away.

Winning prize

- The winners of the give-away will be contacted by mail on the 14th of January 2022. We will draw 5 winners among all the participants.


- The winners will get a gift-card worth a year of tea supply. They will be able to choose among all the different kind of tea we are offering.


- For a logistic efficiency, we kindly ask our winners to pick everything in one or two times.
